About ReWear

Our Mission

ReWear is on a mission to transform the fashion industry by promoting clothing reuse, reducing textile waste, and fostering ethical consumption. We believe in a circular economy where fashion is sustainable, accessible, and conscious.

The Problem

  • Fast fashion produces 10% of global carbon emissions
  • 85% of textiles end up in landfills each year
  • 2,700 liters of water needed for one cotton shirt
  • Textile dyeing is the 2nd largest polluter of clean water

Our Solution

  • Extend clothing lifespan through reuse
  • Curated marketplace for quality pre-loved fashion
  • Carbon-neutral shipping and eco-friendly packaging
  • Transparent impact tracking for every purchase

How We Calculate Impact

Our impact calculations are based on industry-standard metrics for fashion sustainability:

Water Savings

Based on the Water Footprint Network data, producing 1kg of cotton requires approximately 10,000 liters of water. Each item saved from production prevents this water usage.

Carbon Emissions

According to the UN Environment Programme, the fashion industry produces 10% of global carbon emissions. Extending a garment's life by 9 months reduces its carbon footprint by 30%.

Waste Reduction

The Ellen MacArthur Foundation reports that less than 1% of material used to produce clothing is recycled into new clothing. By keeping items in circulation, we dramatically reduce textile waste.
